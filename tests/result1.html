Terms and Conditions

  • 6E Upgrade Service ("Service") is available to all passengers flying domestic segments only at a charge of Rs.2500/- (Rupees Two Thousand Five Hundred Only) inclusive of applicable service tax ("Fee"), per Passenger per Segment. This Fee is non-refundable. For the purpose of these terms and conditions, "Passenger(s)" shall mean and refer to an individual passenger opting for the Service and "Segment" shall mean and refer to single boarding and de-boarding, example Delhi – Mumbai.
  • This Service is offered on a per head basis and in case of multiple passengers on a single PNR, each individual Passenger will be charged this Fee.
  • Under this Service, Passengers can/ are:
    • Modify their booking multiple number of times or cancel the booking, without paying any additional Change or Cancellation fees, as may be applicable and defined in InterGlobe Aviation Limited’s ("IndiGo") then current Conditions of Carriage. Difference in fare may apply.
    • Select the seat of their choice without any charge, including all seats (extra legroom seats and select window and aisle seats) that are otherwise available for pre-assignment at a charge, subject to availability.
    • Entitled to a free meal comprising of a food item and beverage from the available choice on board. Please note that this entitlement will be governed by limitations of choice of food and beverage items that apply to certain short duration flights.
  • In case of cancellation of a booking, after deducting the Fee, the residual amount, if any, will be refunded in accordance with the then current Conditions of Carriage.
  • In case of modification, after deducting the Fee and making the payment for the changed booking, if any residual amount is to be refunded, the same shall be done in accordance with the then current Conditions of Carriage. Further, if there is any excess amount to be paid on account of difference in fare or any applicable charges, the same will be borne by the Passenger.
  • Passengers may book this Service, either through the travel agents or through the website, www.goindigo.in ("Website"), at the time of booking, only.
  • Benefits of the Service can be availed only through the travel agents or through the Website. Thus, Passengers must cancel or modify their booking only through the Website or travel agents.
  • In the event a Passenger cancels or modifies the booking through the Call Centre for any reason, automatic refund of the Change or Cancellation fee will not be done and the applicable Change or Cancellation fee will be charged. Passengers, can however, request for a refund of such Change or Cancellation fee by writing to customer.relations@goindigo.in.
